I am a Professor of Economics at the Toulouse School of Economics (TSE) in France. My research focuses on topics in Development, Labor, and Public Economics.

I am a Research Fellow at the CEPR (Development and Labor), IZA, and EUDN. I am also an invited researcher at J-PAL (Learning for All Initiative and Latin America Office).
