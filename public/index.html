Matteo Bobba

I am currently a Professor of Economics at the Toulouse School of Economics (TSE). Prior to my academic career, I have been an Economist at the Inter-American Development Bank in Washington DC.

I can be reached at matteo.bobba@tse-fr.eu. For information about my teaching activities, including current and past courses, please check my dedicated TSE webpage.

I am a Research Fellow at the CEPR (Development and Labor), IZA, and the EUDN. I am also an invited researcher at JPAL (Learning for All Initiative and Latin America Office).

My research revolves around issues on the spatial distribution of human capital and the functioning of labor markets in low and middle income countries. I am also interested in methodological aspects of policy evaluation, and in particular in combining model-based and design-based approaches.
