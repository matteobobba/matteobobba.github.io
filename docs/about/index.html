I am a Professor of Economics at the Toulouse School of Economics (TSE) in France. Prior to my academic career, I have been an Economist at the Inter-American Development Bank in Washington DC.

I am a Research Fellow at the CEPR (Development and Labor), IZA, and EUDN. I am also an invited researcher at J-PAL (Learning for All Initiative and Latin America Office).

I can be reached at matteo.bobba@tse-fr.eu. My most recent CV is here.

This website is about my academic research, which is at the intersection of Development, Labor and Public Economics. For information about my teaching activities, please check my TSE webpage.
